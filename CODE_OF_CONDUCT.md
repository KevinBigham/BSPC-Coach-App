# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project serves youth sports teams and families. Contributors are expected to communicate professionally, assume good intent, and prioritize safety, privacy, and maintainability.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Use respectful, direct, and constructive language.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Protect private information about swimmers, families, coaches, and teams.
- Prefer clear explanations over gatekeeping.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, safety, and release timing.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private family, swimmer, coach, team, credential, invite, or production data.
- Encouraging unsafe handling of minors' media, attendance, or family information.
- Repeatedly pushing unrelated changes after maintainers define scope.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also block contributors who create safety, privacy, or operational risk for the project.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub.
